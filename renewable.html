Renewable-Energy

Power Project under construction by Sanali Group in Davangere, Karnataka, represents a significant step towards renewable energy expansion in India. This hybrid project combines both wind and solar power, harnessing the natural resources of the region to provide sustainable energy solutions. Karnataka is already a leader in renewable energy production in India, and Davangere, with its favorable climate and land availability, offers an ideal location for this large-scale project.

The project aligns with India's national goals of increasing renewable energy capacity and reducing carbon emissions. Once operational, the 600 MW plant will contribute to reducing reliance on fossil fuels, supporting clean energy transitions, and powering industries and households in the region. Additionally, it is expected to generate employment opportunities during the construction and operational phases and drive economic growth in the local area.

This project also showcases Ameri Group’s commitment to innovation and sustainability in energy infrastructure, positioning the company as a key player in India's green energy future.
